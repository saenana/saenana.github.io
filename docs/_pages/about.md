My name is Sae Na (pronounced Senna), and I'm a mechatronics design engineer.

Born in Seoul, South Korea, I moved to the Philippines when I was 7 years old and spent 11 years there in a small city in the mountain ranges. I flew to Ithaca, NY in 2016 to get my Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering. After graduation, I started working in the customer escalations group at ASML, a photolithography company. While continuing to work full-time, I started to pursue part-time my Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Columbia University with a focus on Robotics and Controls since 2021
​
When I was in the Philippines, I went by the name "Kate Na", which I now no longer use, but some people still know me by.
