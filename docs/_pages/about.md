My name is Sae Na (pronounced Senna), and I'm a mechatronics and robotics engineer with a background in mechanical engineering.

Born in Seoul, I moved to the Philippines in second grade and spent 11 years in Baguio, a small city in the mountain ranges. I flew to Ithaca, NY in 2016 to get my Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering. After graduating, I moved to Connecticut to work as a mechatronics engineer at ASML, a photolithography company. In the summer of 2021, while continuing to work full-time, I started to pursue my Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Columbia University with a focus on Robotics and Controls, which I expect to finish by 2025.

When I was in the Philippines, I went by the name "Kate Na", which I no longer use.
